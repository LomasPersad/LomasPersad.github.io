राम-नाम-मनि-दीप धरु, जीह देहरी द्वार।
तुलसी भीतर बाहिरौ, जौ चाहसि उजियार॥
Ram naam mani deep dharoo jih dehari dwar|
Tulsi bheeter bahrao jo chahasi ujiyar|
If one follows principles laid down by Lord Rama then one can excel in material and spiritual existence

This passage radiates with the essence of devotion and spirituality centered around the chanting of Lord Ram's holy name. It extols the profound significance of repeating this sacred mantra for enlightenment, both in the spiritual and earthly realms.
The name of Shri Ram, like a radiant lamp, illuminates the path through life's struggles, dispelling darkness and fears. It cleanses the heart and mind, offering solace and tranquility amidst life's tumultuous journey.

Yogis, detached from material cravings, find boundless joy in chanting Shri Ram's name. They navigate this world, awake amidst its delusions, contemplating the supreme Brahm, a realm beyond comprehension accessible through the divine vibrations of Ram's name. Invoking Shri Ram's name unravels the path to self-realization, revealing the cosmic consciousness within and connecting it to the omnipresent Brahm, the ultimate reality. Sincere seekers attain extraordinary powers and transcendental insights through unwavering devotion and focused repetition of Lord Ram's name. Even the most distressed souls find solace in chanting Shri Ram's name, transcending their tribulations and trials. This divine name unites seekers of different aspirations—those seeking relief from suffering, those aspiring for material gains, the knowledge seekers, and the enlightened ones—all blessed and elevated by the resonance of Shri Ram's name.

In Kaliyug, despite its imperfections, the potency of Shri Ram's name shines brightly, offering liberation from the cycle of birth and death. It's a time where the act of giving selflessly (Daan) stands as a pivotal pillar of spiritual merit. The message is clear: embracing the name of Shri Ram, practicing it devoutly, brings forth immense spiritual benefits. Gathering in satsang, absorbing scriptures, philosophies, and teachings enriches this spiritual journey. As a new year begins, let resolutions resonate with divine transformation, happiness, and spiritual elevation. The path to enlightenment lies in cherishing the name of Shri Ram—incorporating it into daily life, making it a constant companion. May this year be one of profound spiritual metamorphosis and divine growth for all devotees.
